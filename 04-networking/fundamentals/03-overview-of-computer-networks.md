# Overview Of Computer Networks

## Overview

This section introduces **computer networks** and their role in enabling communication between devices. It covers how systems connect, share information, and operate across **local networks (LAN)** and **wide area networks (WAN)**.

This is useful in real-world environments where understanding how applications communicate and share resources is essential. **Computer networking** forms the foundation for **internet connectivity**, **application communication**, and modern infrastructure.

---

## Commands

This section is concept-focused, but these tools will later be used to apply your knowledge:

- `ping` → Test **connectivity between devices**  
- `traceroute` → Trace the **path between networks**  
- `ip` → View **network interfaces and IP addresses**  
- `netstat` / `ss` → Inspect **network connections and ports**  

---

## Key Takeaways

- **Computer networks** connect devices to share data and resources  
- **LAN and WAN** represent different network scopes (local vs global)  
- Networks enable **communication**, **resource sharing**, and **application connectivity**  
- Networking is essential for **internet usage**, cloud systems, and infrastructure  
- Understanding networks is key to working in **DevOps environments**
